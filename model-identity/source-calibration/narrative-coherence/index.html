Narrative Coherence

Narrative Coherence describes how consistently a person connects experience, memory, interpretation, and self-understanding into a stable inner storyline.

How to read it

The result label summarizes the current coherence pattern. The supporting score is shown on a 0 to 10 scale and determines the status indicator.

Why it matters

The model uses Narrative Coherence to decide how strongly it should link memories, detect recurring patterns, preserve continuity, and support long-term reflection.

Important boundary

Narrative Coherence is not a diagnosis or identity label. It is a private calibration signal that helps the model understand continuity and integration over time.
