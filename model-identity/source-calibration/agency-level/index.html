Agency Level

Agency Level describes how strongly a person’s current answers indicate self-directed action, choice, and follow-through.

How to read it

The result label summarizes the current agency pattern. The supporting score is shown on a 0 to 10 scale and determines the status indicator.

Why it matters

The model uses Agency Level to decide how much it should emphasize initiative, next steps, pacing, and action-oriented reflection.

Important boundary

Agency Level is not a judgment of ability or character. It is a private calibration signal that helps the model respond with better timing and support.
